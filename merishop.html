Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 20, 2025

Welcome to Meri Shop (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Your email address (via Gmail account chooser).
  • When you entered your password for account create purposes. This password is encrypted securely before storing in our backend.
  • Shop Information: Shop name,shop image(profile),posts, owner name, city, state, address,contact( phone number or email ), and shop status (Open, Closed, or any custom status text you choose).
  • App Usage Data: Favorites stored locally on your device (using AsyncStorage), login tokens, and user reports.

2. Device Permissions

Our app only requires Internet access permission to communicate with our servers and upload data securely. We do not request any permissions to access your device’s photos, media, or camera directly. When you choose to upload images, the app uses your device’s native photo picker, which allows you to select photos without granting the app direct access to your media or storage. This ensures your privacy and control over your files. We only access the photos or files that you explicitly select and do not access or store any other data from your device.

3. Profile View Count

The app tracks how many times a user's profile is viewed. This view count is stored securely in our database and is only visible to the respective user. We do not share this data with any third parties.

4. Data Storage and Backend

All shop-related data including shop name,shop image(profile),posts, owner name, city, state, address,contact(phone number or email), view count and status or note is stored securely on our backend servers hosted on AWS and saved in a MongoDB database.

This information is publicly visible to all users of the app when browsing or searching shops.

The status field is flexible, allowing shop owners to enter any text describing their shop’s current state.

Uploaded images are securely stored on our cloud storage (such as AWS S3 and MongoDB) and are not shared with any third parties.

5. Account Sign-In and Permissions

When you sign up using your Gmail account, the app opens the Google Account Chooser modal directly without requesting any additional device permissions such as location or contacts.

This ensures a quick, secure, and privacy-friendly sign-up process.

We only access your email address for authentication purposes.

6.User-Generated Content (UGC)

Our app allows users to enter and upload content that is publicly visible to other users. This includes:

  • Shop name,shop image(profile),posts, owner name
  • contact(Email address or phone number)
  • City, state, and address
  • Custom shop status
  • Profile images

All of this information is provided voluntarily by users when creating or updating their shop profile. Since this content is publicly visible to other users, please ensure it does not contain any private, sensitive, abusive, or false information.

We reserve the right to remove any user-generated content that violates our content policy or applicable laws. Users who repeatedly post inappropriate content may have their accounts suspended or removed.

If you find any content that violates our terms, please report it to: merishopapp.in@gmail.com

7. Favorites and Local Storage

You can save your favorite shops locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

Favorites data is stored only on your device. It is automatically deleted when you log out, and you can also manually remove it anytime from within the app.

Your authentication token is also removed securely on logout to protect your privacy.

8. Data Editing and Deletion

After creating a shop, you can edit certain information such as:

  • Shop Name
  • Owner Name
  • posts
  • City
  • Address
  • Contact (Email or Phone number)
  • shop Status (You can update or leave it empty)
  • Profile Image (You can replace the existing image)

Deletion of your Gmail account data or shop can be performed directly from the Settings section within the app. If you wish to permanently remove your data from our servers, please go to the Home screen, tap the top-right corner menu,click advance Setting and select the "Delete Account" option. Follow the confirmation steps to complete the process.

Once your account is deleted, all associated data including your Gmail login, shop information, and any related personal content is permanently removed from our system. We do not store or retain any of your data after successful deletion.

Note: Simply uninstalling or deleting the app from your phone does not remove your account or data from our servers. Your account will remain active unless you delete it yourself using the in-app option as explained above.

We are fully committed to data privacy and comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding user data collection, access, modification, and deletion. Your information is handled with utmost security and respect.

If you face any issue about deletion you can contact on merishopapp.in@gmail.com .

9. User Reporting and Policy Enforcement

Users can report any shop or user for inappropriate content or policy violations by contacting our team via email(merishopapp.in@gmail.com).

All reports are sent securely to our team via email.

Our dedicated moderation team reviews all reports and takes necessary action including warnings, suspension, or removal of violating content.

This ensures a safe and trustworthy environment for all users.

10. Logout and Data Removal

Logging out removes all local favorite shops and your login authentication token from your device.

This prevents unauthorized access and keeps your data safe.

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our app does not use cookies. However, it may use local storage (such as AsyncStorage) to store user preferences or login information securely on your device.
