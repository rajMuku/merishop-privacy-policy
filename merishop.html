Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2025

MeriShop is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by the MeriShop app.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Information: Name, email, shop name, address, city, state, and optional profile image.
  • Status Notes: Any note you write for your shop.
  • Device Info: Basic device data for security and analytics (non-personal).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your shop profile and help customers find your business.
  • To allow communication between users and shops.
  • To improve user experience and provide app support.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. All data is used only within the MeriShop platform.

4. Data Security

We use secure methods to store and transfer your data. However, no app is 100% secure, so please use the app responsibly.

5. User Control

You can update or delete your profile information anytime from within the app. For complete deletion, contact us at the email below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Updates will be reflected here with a new "Effective Date."

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at: support@merishopapp.com
