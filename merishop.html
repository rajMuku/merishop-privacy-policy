Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2025

Welcome to Your Shop App (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Your email (via Gmail account chooser).
  • Shop Information: Shop name, owner name, city, state, address, and shop status (Open, Closed, or any custom status text you choose).
  • App Usage Data: Favorites stored locally on your device (using AsyncStorage), login tokens, and user reports.

2. Data Storage and Backend

All shop-related data including shop name, owner name, city, state, address, and status is stored securely on our backend servers hosted on AWS and saved in a MongoDB database.

This information is publicly visible to all users of the app when browsing or searching shops.

The status field is flexible, allowing shop owners to enter any text describing their shop’s current state.

3. Google Account Sign-In and Permissions

When you sign up using your Gmail account, the app opens the Google Account Chooser modal directly without requesting any additional device permissions such as location or contacts.

This ensures a quick, secure, and privacy-friendly sign-up process.

We only access your email address for authentication purposes.

4. Favorites and Local Storage

You can save your favorite shops locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

Favorites data is stored only on your device and is deleted automatically when you log out.

Your authentication token is also removed securely on logout to protect your privacy.

5. Data Editing and Deletion

You can edit your personal and shop information anytime within the app.

To delete your Gmail account data from our servers, a formal request must be sent via your registered Gmail address to ensure secure verification.

We comply with all applicable laws regarding data privacy and deletion.

6. User Reporting and Policy Enforcement

Users can report any shop or user for inappropriate content or policy violations directly via the app.

All reports are sent securely to our team via email.

Our dedicated moderation team reviews all reports and takes necessary action including warnings, suspension, or removal of violating content.

This ensures a safe and trustworthy environment for all users.

7. Logout and Data Removal

Logging out removes all local favorite shops and your login authentication token from your device.

This prevents unauthorized access and keeps your data safe.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our app may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience.

Cookies help remember your preferences and app settings.

You can disable cookies on your device, but some app features may not work properly.

9. Compliance with Laws and Children’s Privacy

We follow all relevant laws and regulations including Indian data protection laws.

Our app is not intended for children under 18 years old.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

10. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Information is shared only with trusted service providers who help us operate the app and maintain servers, under strict confidentiality.

11. Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally.

Updates will be posted within the app and take effect immediately.

Please review periodically.
